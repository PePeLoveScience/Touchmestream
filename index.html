Before joining

What users should check

Before taking part, read the partner page carefully so you understand exactly what is required.

  • Age, country, device, and entry requirements.
  • Reward description, claim steps, and availability.
  • Privacy policy and how submitted information may be used.
  • Any limits, deadlines, or conditions shown by the partner.
  • Whether the promotion is void where prohibited.
Safe participation

What to avoid

Do not continue if a page feels unsafe, unclear, or asks for information that does not match the promotion.

  • Requests for passwords, one-time verification codes, or banking PINs.
  • Claims that every visitor will receive the same reward.
  • Unexplained fees or requirements that were not shown clearly.
  • Missing rules, missing privacy details, or unclear sponsor information.
  • Fake urgency, fake scarcity, or confusing instructions.
Important disclaimer

Promotions are not guaranteed

TouchMeStream does not decide who qualifies, who receives a reward, or how a partner promotion is fulfilled. The partner page and official rules control the final requirements.

We may receive compensation when visitors click partner links or participate in third-party offers. This does not change the rules, eligibility requirements, or final outcome.

Common questions

Is TouchMeStream the sponsor of every promotion?
No. TouchMeStream is a discovery and information website. Partner promotions are operated by third-party advertisers, brands, or promotion providers.
Do I always get a reward after playing or answering questions?
No. A game, quiz, or question flow does not guarantee a reward. Eligibility, availability, and claim requirements depend on the partner promotion rules.
Why do some promotions ask questions?
Some advertisers use questions, surveys, or interactive steps to match users with available campaigns or confirm eligibility before showing the next steps.
What should I read before submitting information?
Read the official rules, eligibility requirements, privacy policy, reward details, and claim instructions shown on the partner page.
